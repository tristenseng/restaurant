About Us

Welcome to Mad Eats

Located in the Bluff Heights neighborhood of Long Beach, Mad Eats serves Breakfast, Lunch, Happy Hour and Dinner, featuring a menu of quintessential dishes that honor the classics of American dining created by our chefs.

Local coffee roasters such as Rose Park Roasters brew our coffee all day. Vegetarian and vegan options are available.
